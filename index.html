What We Offer: Services and Amenities

We’ve worked hard to make Hunnewell’s Cottages more than just a rental: it’s a destination. Here’s what you’ll find when you stay with us:

  • Wedding Venue & Event Hosting: Our lakeside property is a beautiful setting for weddings, family reunions, and private events. We also provide event hosting and coordination, making it easier for you to plan every detail.
  • Lakeview Cottages: Each cottage is designed with comfort in mind and offers stunning views of the surrounding lakes. Imagine sipping coffee in the morning while the Florida sun reflects off the water.
  • Complementary Boat Access: Take advantage of our complimentary boat availability and explore the peaceful waters of Lake Byrd or Lake Brentwood. Fishing, kayaking, and leisurely rides are all right outside your door.
  • Housekeeping Services: We provide professional housekeeping services so your time here is focused on relaxation, not cleaning.
  • Outdoor Fun: Enjoy our private beach area, fishing spots, barbecue grills, picnic tables, and fire pits.
  • Nearby Adventures: When you want to explore beyond the lake, we’re just a short drive from Sebring International Raceway, popular golf courses, and family attractions like Legoland and Disney World.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I rent this for a wedding venue?
    Yes! We do ask for at least six months’ advance notice. We also offer discounts on fireworks and flowers to make your big day even more memorable.
  • Can I book for the long term?
    Absolutely. We offer discounts for extended stays, making it affordable and convenient to enjoy the beauty of Avon Park for weeks or even months at a time.
  • Are you pet-friendly?
    Yes, we welcome pets under 20 pounds. We know pets are part of the family, and we want them to feel just as comfortable as you do.
  • Can I do activities on the lake from the property?
    Yes. Boats are available (with limited availability), and you’re free to enjoy fishing, boating, and other water activities right from our property.
  • Do you have any smoking rooms?
    No, all of our rooms are smoke-free for the enjoyment of all of our guests. There are designated smoking areas throughout the area. If you do smoke in the room, there will be $150 smoke violation fee.
  • Do you have housekeeping services?
    Yes! We have housekeeping to make sure your stay is as comfortable and care-free as possible. However, if you check out and the room is left in an excessive mess, there could be a charge of up to $150.

Experience Avon Park and Central Florida

Our location is one of our greatest advantages. Avon Park is perfectly positioned in Central Florida, meaning you’re close to some of the state’s biggest attractions while still enjoying a peaceful, small-town atmosphere. Within minutes, you can play a round at Highlands Ridge Golf Club or explore the Avon Park Depot Museum for a taste of local history. A short drive south takes you to Sebring, home of the world-famous Sebring International Raceway. For family fun, Legoland Florida is less than an hour away, and if you’re up for a day trip, Walt Disney World Resort is about 90 minutes from your cottage door.
But you don’t have to go far to find beauty. The surrounding lakes, citrus groves, and wildlife of Highlands County create a backdrop that feels authentically Floridian. Whether you’re fishing at sunrise, walking along the shore, or just soaking in the warm climate, you’ll understand why so many guests return to Avon Park year after year.
