Rooms at Hunnewell’s Cottages: Cottage Rentals in Avon Park, FL

At Hunnewell’s Cottages, we’ve created a place where comfort, convenience, and the beauty of Avon Park come together. Nestled between Lake Byrd and Lake Brentwood, our cottages are more than just a place to sleep. They’re a true getaway where you can unwind, recharge, and enjoy the Florida lifestyle. We’re proud to be family-owned and operated, and we take great care in providing the kind of personal service that makes your stay special. Whether you’re booking a short weekend escape or a longer cottage stay, we have accommodations designed to fit your needs.

We have a variety of cottages to choose from to make your stay as comfortable as possible. Stay in one of our newly decorated and updated Lakeside rooms or choose one of very cute and cozy front cottages. Before booking your stay please take a look at our terms & conditions.

Cottage Stays Tailored to You

Every traveler is different, which is why we offer a variety of cottage rentals to match your style. Looking for something cozy and budget-friendly? Our Front Cottages give you all the essentials at a great value. Want to wake up to the sound of water and step directly onto our private beach? Our Lakeside Cottages are made for that. No matter which option you choose, you’ll enjoy well-maintained, updated spaces along with modern conveniences and thoughtful touches.

Front Cottages: Comfort and Value

Our Front Cottages are ideal if you want comfort at an affordable rate. These charming units feature 40-inch televisions, cable, and high-speed internet, so you’ll have plenty of entertainment after a day exploring Avon Park. Each unit includes a microwave, coffee pot, and refrigerator, making it easy to prepare quick meals or snacks. Several also feature a two-burner stove for more flexibility.

Guests in the Front Cottages enjoy full access to our charcoal barbecue grills and picnic tables, perfect for gathering with friends or family under the warm Florida sky. And while they’re just a short walk from the beach, you’ll find they offer nearly all the same conveniences as our Lakeside Cottages at a lower price point.

Amenities include: 40-inch cable TV, Microwave and coffee maker, Mini fridge and select kitchenettes, High-speed internet, Charcoal barbecue grills and picnic areas

Lakeside Cottages: Steps from the Beach

If you want to be right on the water, our Lakeside Cottages are the perfect choice. These updated units are just steps away from our private beach and provide stunning lake views. Each cottage features a 60-inch television, cable, and high-speed internet for your convenience.

The kitchens come fully equipped with a refrigerator, two burners, microwave, toaster oven, and coffee maker, which is ideal for longer stays. Instead of charcoal, every Lakeside Cottage has its own private gas barbecue grill, so you can cook outdoors without the mess of lighter fluid. Add in on-demand hot water and split air conditioning units, and you’ll have everything you need for a relaxing and comfortable stay.

Amenities include: 60-inch cable TV, Full kitchenette with refrigerator, microwave, toaster oven, and two burners, Coffee maker, Private gas barbecue grill with burners, Private beach access, On-demand hot water and air conditioning

The Beauty of Avon Park and Beyond

Staying with us means enjoying more than just the cottage: you’ll also have access to the charm and attractions of Avon Park and Highlands County! Known as the “City of Charm,” Avon Park is surrounded by lakes, citrus groves, and rolling landscapes that showcase the best of Central Florida.

Spend your day fishing or kayaking right from our property, then head into town to enjoy local dining, shops, or a round of golf at Highlands Ridge Golf Club. Just south in Sebring, you’ll find the Sebring International Raceway, a world-class destination for motorsports fans. For family outings, Legoland Florida is less than an hour away, and Walt Disney World is about a 90-minute drive. Whether you want a peaceful retreat or easy access to big attractions, our location makes it possible.

Housekeeping Services and Guest Comfort

We know how important it is to truly relax when you’re away from home. That’s why we offer housekeeping services to help keep your cottage fresh and clean during your stay. You’ll spend less time worrying about tidying up and more time enjoying the water, the sunshine, and everything Avon Park has to offer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide long-term stays?
Yes, we welcome long-term guests and offer discounts to make extended stays more affordable.

Can I book a cottage for just a weekend?
Absolutely. We’re happy to host short-term guests as well as longer vacations.

Are pets allowed?
Yes, we welcome small pets under 20 pounds so you don’t have to leave your furry family members behind.

Do the cottages have internet access?
Yes, both our Front and Lakeside Cottages have high-speed internet for your convenience.

Do the Lakeside Cottages include private beach access?
Yes, Lakeside Cottage guests enjoy exclusive access to our private beach.
