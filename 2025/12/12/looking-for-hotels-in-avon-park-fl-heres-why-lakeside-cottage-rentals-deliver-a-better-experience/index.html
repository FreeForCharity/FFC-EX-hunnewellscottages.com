If you’re planning a trip to Central Florida and searching for the best hotels in Avon Park, FL, it’s time to consider an alternative that offers more comfort, charm, and character than a typical hotel room. A Unique Stay with Lakeside Views and Private Access

Unlike standard hotels in Avon Park, FL, our cottages are nestled between Lake Byrd and Lake Brentwood, offering serene views and direct access to the water. Imagine waking up to the sound of birds and sipping your morning coffee while looking out over a calm lake, just steps from your door. Our cottages give you private beach access, something that no chain hotel in the area can offer.

Whether you’re on a romantic getaway, a family trip, or a quiet solo retreat, having your own space to unwind makes a big difference. The peaceful surroundings and natural beauty of our lakeside property offer a refreshing contrast to the hustle of traditional hotel experiences.

Complimentary Outdoor Adventures for Every Guest

One of the most memorable parts of staying at Hunnewell’s Cottages is the complimentary access to boating, kayaking, and fishing. When you book with us, you’re not just getting a place to sleep; you’re gaining access to a full outdoor experience. Close to Everything, Yet a World Away

Our location in Avon Park makes us an ideal base for exploring some of Florida’s most popular attractions. We’re just a short drive from Legoland and Disney World, making day trips easy for families. Car enthusiasts and sports fans will appreciate that we’re only minutes away from Sebring International Raceway, which hosts events year-round and is one of the most well-known tracks in the state.

For golf lovers, there are multiple courses nearby, including Highlands Ridge Golf Club. History buffs can stop by the Avon Park Depot Museum, and nature lovers can take scenic drives through citrus groves and wildlife preserves. Whether you’re looking for excitement or relaxation, our central location offers the best of both worlds.

Despite being close to all these attractions, our property feels completely removed from the crowds. Guests often tell us how surprised they are to find such tranquility so close to major highways and destinations. That’s the beauty of Avon Park; it’s a hidden gem in Central Florida, and Hunnewell’s Cottages lets you experience it at its finest.
