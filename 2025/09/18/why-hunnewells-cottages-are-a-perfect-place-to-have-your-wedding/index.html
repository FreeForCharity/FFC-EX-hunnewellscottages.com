Why Hunnewell’s Cottages Are a Perfect Place to Have Your Wedding

September 18, 2025 by James Deapen

At Hunnewell’s Cottages, we believe that the right setting makes all the difference when it comes to celebrating love. Nestled on Lake Brentwood in Avon Park, Florida, our property offers the perfect balance between natural beauty and cozy comfort. Couples searching for a wedding venue that feels intimate yet unforgettable will discover just how magical our lake view truly is. The sparkling water of Lake Brentwood provides a stunning backdrop for ceremonies, photographs, and receptions. It is the kind of scenery that doesn’t need much decoration because it naturally brings romance into every frame. Whether the sun is rising with golden light or setting with soft pastels, the lake’s reflections turn your vows into unforgettable memories.

What makes our space even more special is how peaceful and private it feels. Just seven miles north of Sebring and right off Highway 27, our location is convenient for your guests while still offering the seclusion couples crave. You can exchange vows lakeside, enjoy an open-air reception under the stars, and retreat to one of our cottages when the night winds down. Our wedding venue combines the convenience of central Florida with the intimacy of a tucked-away escape, making it a choice you and your guests will treasure forever.

Comfort and Hospitality in Every Cottage

Beyond the lake view, one of the things that truly sets us apart is the comfort of our cottages. We take pride in making sure every detail contributes to the relaxing and home-like atmosphere that guests deserve. Every cottage on our property is carefully maintained, and we’ve upgraded our amenities to ensure that your stay is seamless. Each space is now equipped with split air conditioning units, which are so quiet they are nearly silent. This makes them perfect for those moments of calm before and after your wedding celebrations. Couples, families, and wedding parties can enjoy both comfort and privacy without interruption.

In addition, we have invested in European tankless natural gas hot water heaters, which means no one will ever run out of hot water. After a long day of celebrating, you can relax with a hot shower at any time without worry. We’ve combined the charm of cottage rental living with the convenience of modern amenities, ensuring your guests feel cared for throughout their stay. Hospitality is at the core of what we do, and we are proud to be known for creating the kind of warm, welcoming atmosphere that makes weddings here even more memorable. When your family and friends arrive, they won’t just feel like guests; they’ll feel like part of ours.

Unique Perks That Make Weddings Extra Special

Every wedding at Hunnewell’s Cottages is unique, and we love helping couples add those personal touches that make the celebration truly their own. One of the ways we go above and beyond is by providing opportunities to elevate your wedding with unique, cost-friendly extras. Because we also run a seasonal fireworks retail business, we can offer our couples access to spectacular fireworks displays at an unbelievably low cost. For just $500, you can light up the sky above Lake Brentwood in celebration of your big day. Few experiences are as memorable as sharing your first dance with fireworks reflecting on the water behind you.

We also have strong wholesale connections for fresh flowers, which means you can create breathtaking arrangements and décor without the steep price tags often attached to weddings. These partnerships allow us to extend incredible value to our couples while maintaining the beauty and elegance they envision for their day. From bouquets and centerpieces to floral arches set against the lake, we can help ensure that your wedding design is stunning without straining your budget. Our team cares deeply about creating experiences that go beyond expectations, and we take pride in working with you to add touches of magic that feel personal and thoughtful.

A Central Florida Location That Offers Convenience

Choosing the right wedding venue often means balancing beauty with accessibility. Hunnewell’s Cottages offers the best of both worlds. We are centrally located in Florida, surrounded by major cities yet far enough out to feel like a retreat. Our property is about an hour’s drive from Universal Studios and just 30 minutes from Legoland, making it easy for your out-of-town guests to explore Florida’s attractions while they’re here. At the same time, our cottages allow everyone to return to a peaceful, relaxing environment after their adventures. This makes us an ideal option not only for weddings but also for extended stays and family vacations surrounding your event.

For guests traveling within Florida, our location off Highway 27 makes us simple to find and convenient to reach. Whether your guests are coming from Tampa, Orlando, or the surrounding towns, they’ll appreciate that they don’t have to travel far to celebrate with you. Our proximity to Sebring also means you can take advantage of local services and vendors, ensuring every detail of your day is covered. At Hunnewell’s Cottages, we provide the central location, the lake view setting, and the cottage rental hospitality that makes everyone’s experience enjoyable from start to finish.

Creating Memories with Exceptional Value

We know weddings are one of the most important days of your life, and we are committed to offering more than just a place to host it. Our focus is always on creating value for our couples by combining charm, comfort, and affordability. For example, when you book our weekly rate for your stay, you receive your seventh night free. This allows you to extend your wedding celebration into a full getaway without additional costs. Many couples enjoy arriving early to prepare and then staying after to relax as newlyweds, and this option makes it simple and cost-effective.

By offering flexible options for lodging, affordable extras like fireworks displays, and our strong vendor connections for flowers, we create an experience where beauty meets budget in perfect harmony. We believe that no couple should have to sacrifice quality or atmosphere in order to stay within their means. Our dedication to customer care means that we work with you at every stage, making sure your vision comes to life in ways that are affordable, practical, and heartfelt. Every couple who celebrates here leaves with more than just photographs; they leave with memories of feeling cared for and valued.

Conclusion

At Hunnewell’s Cottages, we know that your wedding is more than an event. It is a milestone in your life and a memory you’ll cherish forever. That is why we strive to provide not just a wedding venue, but an entire experience built on beauty, comfort, and thoughtful hospitality. With our lake view setting, cozy and upgraded cottages, unique perks like fireworks and wholesale flowers, and convenient central Florida location, we offer everything you need to create a wedding that feels magical without being overwhelming.

As a family-owned business, we care deeply about our couples and their loved ones. Our mission is to ensure that from the first tour of our property to the last moment of your celebration, you feel supported, welcomed, and at home. When you choose Hunnewell’s Cottages for your wedding, you’re not just booking a venue. You’re choosing a team that will treat your special day as if it were our own. We would be honored to host your love story on the shores of Lake Brentwood, where unforgettable memories are made every day.
