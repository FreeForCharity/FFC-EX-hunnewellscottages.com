Amenities

When you stay at Hunnewell’s Cottages in Avon Park, right outside your door is Lake Brentwood. Check out our game room and the wonderful gazebo at the end of our dock.
Learn about our community rules and cancellation policies.

Game Room


Not only does our game room have a pool table, it also has laundry machines and a television. Be entertained while you clean your clothes. You can grab a cue, wrack ’em up, and start putting them away while your swimsuit and towel dry from a fun day on the beach. There’s never a dull moment at Hunnewell’s Cottages!
